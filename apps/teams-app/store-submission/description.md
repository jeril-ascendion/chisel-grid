# ChiselGrid Teams App — Store Listing

## Short Description (80 chars max)
AI-powered engineering knowledge base with voice capture and content generation

## Full Description
ChiselGrid brings your engineering knowledge base directly into Microsoft Teams.

**Key Features:**

- **Knowledge Bot** — Ask questions and get instant answers from your team's published articles. The AI bot searches the knowledge base and cites sources automatically.

- **Content Creation** — Start AI-generated article drafts right from Teams. Track draft progress as AI agents write, review, and optimize content.

- **Review Workflow** — Admins receive review requests as Adaptive Cards. Approve or reject articles with feedback without leaving Teams.

- **Channel Notifications** — Automatically post new articles to configured Teams channels. Keep your team informed as new content is published.

- **Meeting Intelligence** — Automatically transcribe and process Teams meeting recordings into structured knowledge articles.

- **M365 Copilot Integration** — Search your engineering knowledge base from M365 Copilot in Word, Outlook, and Teams.

- **SSO Authentication** — Seamless single sign-on with your organization's Azure AD. No separate login required.

**How It Works:**

1. Install the ChiselGrid app in Teams
2. Chat with the Knowledge Bot to search articles
3. Create new content via voice or text
4. Review and approve articles from Adaptive Cards
5. Configure channel notifications for your team

**Powered by:**
- AWS Bedrock (Claude AI) for intelligent content generation
- Amazon Transcribe for voice-to-text
- Adaptive Cards for rich interactive experiences

**Requirements:**
- Microsoft Teams desktop, web, or mobile
- ChiselGrid subscription (Starter, Professional, or Enterprise)
- Azure AD for SSO authentication
