ChiselGrid Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use ChiselGrid through Microsoft Teams, we collect:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and Azure AD object ID from your organization's directory.
  • Content data: Articles, drafts, voice recordings, and meeting transcripts that you create or upload.
  • Usage data: Search queries, article views, and feature usage for analytics and improvement.
  • Teams context: Channel IDs and team membership for channel notification routing.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the ChiselGrid knowledge base service
  • To authenticate you via Azure AD single sign-on
  • To generate AI-powered content using AWS Bedrock
  • To transcribe voice recordings and meeting audio
  • To send notifications to configured Teams channels
  • To improve our service through anonymized usage analytics

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in AWS infrastructure in the ap-southeast-1 (Singapore) region. We use:

  • Aurora PostgreSQL with encryption at rest for structured data
  • S3 with server-side encryption for media and documents
  • AWS Secrets Manager for credential storage
  • TLS 1.2+ for all data in transit

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Data is processed by:

  • AWS Bedrock: For AI content generation (data not used for model training)
  • Amazon Transcribe: For voice-to-text conversion
  • Microsoft Graph API: For Teams integration features

5. Multi-Tenant Isolation

ChiselGrid enforces strict tenant isolation. Your organization's data is never accessible to other tenants. Row-level security policies in the database ensure complete data separation.

6. Data Retention

Content is retained as long as your subscription is active. Upon cancellation, data is retained for 30 days before permanent deletion. You may request immediate deletion at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact privacy@chiselgrid.com for data requests.

8. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at privacy@chiselgrid.com.
